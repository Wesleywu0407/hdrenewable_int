# Chapter 1.1 — Queensland Renewable Energy: A Slow Start, Then Acceleration
# 第 1.1 章 — 昆士蘭再生能源：起步緩慢，隨後加速

**Prepared for:** HDRE Taiwan research report
**Data source:** OpenElectricity API (openelectricity.org.au)
**Generated:** 2026-06-22

> **Narrative correction / 敘事修正:** This chapter was originally framed around QLD renewable
> *stagnation* relative to a NSW *surge*. **The data does not support that thesis** and we have
> not forced it. In reality QLD started slowly (2020–21) and then **accelerated sharply in
> 2024–25**, converging with NSW at ~32% renewable share. The honest, data-led story is told
> below. 本章原以「昆士蘭再生能源停滯、新南威爾斯爆發」為架構，但**資料並不支持此論點**，我們未予強行
> 套用。實際上昆士蘭起步緩慢（2020–21 年），隨後於 **2024–25 年大幅加速**，再生能源佔比已追平
> 新南威爾斯，約達 32%。

## Section 1 — QLD Renewable Share vs Peers (Fig 1)
## 第一節 — 昆士蘭再生能源佔比與其他州比較（圖 1）

**EN.** *Context:* Fig 1 tracks each state's monthly renewable share over the last ~24 months,
with QLD highlighted. *Key statistics:* QLD rose from **19.2%** to **32.2%**; NSW moved from
**22.0%** to **32.3%** — the two have **converged**, not diverged. SA (~74%) and TAS (~99%) sit
in a different structural league. *Interpretation:* QLD's catch-up coincides with a wave of
utility solar and wind reaching commercial operation, even as the state's coal fleet keeps the
*absolute* renewable share capped near one-third. The widely cited "QLD is falling behind"
narrative reflects 2020–22 conditions, not the current trajectory. *HDRE implication:* QLD is a
fast-moving but still coal-anchored market — renewable + firming assets entering now ride a
rising-share trend rather than a saturated one.

**繁中.** *脈絡：* 圖 1 追蹤各州近約 24 個月的每月再生能源佔比，並突顯昆士蘭。*關鍵數據：*
昆士蘭由 **19.2%** 升至 **32.2%**；新南威爾斯由 **22.0%** 升至 **32.3%**——兩者**已趨同**而非分化。
南澳（約 74%）與塔斯馬尼亞（約 99%）則屬不同結構層級。*解讀：* 昆士蘭的追趕，與一波大型太陽能及
風電投入商轉同步發生，但其燃煤機隊仍使*絕對*再生佔比受限於約三分之一。常被引用的「昆士蘭落後」
敘事反映的是 2020–22 年情況，而非當前軌跡。*對 HDRE 的意涵：* 昆士蘭是快速變動但仍以燃煤為錨的
市場——現在進場的再生能源與 firming 資產，搭上的是佔比上升而非飽和的趨勢。

## Section 2 — QLD Fuel Mix Evolution (Fig 2)
## 第二節 — 昆士蘭發電組合演變（圖 2）

**EN.** *Context:* Fig 2 stacks QLD's monthly generation by fuel over the last 24 months.
*Key statistics:* Coal still supplies the majority of QLD generation (~57–59% across the window),
with utility+rooftop solar the largest renewable contributor (~24%) and wind near 9%. *Interpretation:*
The mix is still firmly coal-anchored — QLD has the most coal-dependent generation profile of the
mainland NEM states — but the renewable wedge visibly thickens in the 2025–26 summers as new solar
comes online. *HDRE implication:* the persistence of coal in QLD's mix means the *displacement*
opportunity (and the firming required as coal retires) is larger here than in NSW or VIC.

**繁中.** *脈絡：* 圖 2 以堆疊面積呈現昆士蘭近 24 個月各燃料每月發電量。*關鍵數據：* 燃煤仍供應
昆士蘭多數電量（期間約 57–59%），大型＋屋頂太陽能為最大再生來源（約 24%），風電接近 9%。
*解讀：* 發電組合仍以燃煤為主——昆士蘭是 NEM 本島各州中最依賴燃煤者——但隨新太陽能上線，
再生能源區塊在 2025–26 年夏季明顯增厚。*對 HDRE 的意涵：* 燃煤在昆士蘭組合中的持續存在，意味
此地的*替代*機會（以及燃煤除役時所需的 firming）比新南威爾斯或維多利亞更大。

## Section 3 — Utility Solar vs Wind Buildout (Fig 3)
## 第三節 — 大型太陽能與風電建設（圖 3）

**EN.** *Context:* Fig 3 plots QLD's cumulative installed utility-solar and wind capacity from
facility commissioning dates. *Key statistics:* Utility solar has reached **~5,111 MW**, well
ahead of wind at **~2,647 MW** — solar is roughly **1.9×** wind. *Interpretation:* QLD's
renewable build has been solar-led, exploiting the state's high irradiance, while wind has grown
more slowly (fewer high-quality, transmission-connected wind zones developed to date). A
solar-skewed fleet concentrates output in the middle of the day. *HDRE implication:* the solar
skew is precisely what creates midday oversupply and a steep evening ramp — the commercial basis
for storage and dispatchable firming, which is QLD's clearest near-term gap.

**繁中.** *脈絡：* 圖 3 以設施商轉日期繪出昆士蘭累計大型太陽能與風電裝置容量。*關鍵數據：*
大型太陽能已達 **約 5,111 MW**，大幅領先風電的 **約 2,647 MW**——太陽能約為風電的 **1.9 倍**。
*解讀：* 昆士蘭的再生建設以太陽能為主，善用該州高日照；風電成長較慢（迄今開發的優質、且具輸電
連接的風場較少）。偏向太陽能的機隊使出力集中於日中。*對 HDRE 的意涵：* 此太陽能偏向正是造成
日中供給過剩與傍晚陡升的成因——也是儲能與可調度 firming 的商業基礎，為昆士蘭最明確的近期缺口。

## Section 4 — Year-on-Year Capacity Additions (Fig 4) — the centerpiece
## 第四節 — 逐年新增容量（圖 4）— 核心圖表

**EN.** *Context:* Fig 4 compares renewable MW commissioned per year in QLD, NSW and VIC, 2020–2026.
*Key statistics:* QLD added just **~405 MW across 2020–21 combined**, but **~2,851 MW across
2024–25** — a roughly **7× step-up**. In 2025 QLD added **1,535 MW**, *more* than NSW's 978 MW.
*Interpretation:* this is the empirical refutation of the "stagnation" thesis. QLD's early-decade
lull (transmission constraints, project-pipeline timing) gave way to a strong 2022 and a
record-setting 2024–25. The 2026 bar is a partial year and should not be read as a downturn.
*HDRE implication:* QLD is now one of the most active renewable build markets in the NEM —
relevant for project competition, but also confirming strong demand for connection and firming.

**繁中.** *脈絡：* 圖 4 比較 2020–2026 年昆士蘭、新南威爾斯、維多利亞每年商轉的再生能源容量（MW）。
*關鍵數據：* 昆士蘭 **2020–21 兩年合計僅新增約 405 MW**，但 **2024–25 兩年合計約 2,851 MW**——
約 **7 倍** 的躍升。2025 年昆士蘭新增 **1,535 MW**，*高於*新南威爾斯的 978 MW。*解讀：* 這正是對
「停滯」論點的實證反駁。昆士蘭十年初期的停頓（輸電限制、專案管線時程）已讓位給強勁的 2022 年與
創紀錄的 2024–25 年。2026 長條為不完整年度，不應解讀為下滑。*對 HDRE 的意涵：* 昆士蘭如今是
NEM 中最活躍的再生建設市場之一——關乎專案競爭，也印證對併網與 firming 的強勁需求。

## Section 5 — Coal Plants Still Operating (Fig 5)
## 第五節 — 仍運轉的燃煤電廠（圖 5）

**EN.** *Context:* Fig 5 ranks QLD's operating black-coal plants by capacity and expected
retirement year. *Key statistics:* **8 plants, ~8,165 MW** remain operating. The earliest exits
are **Gladstone (1,680 MW, ~2029)** and **Callide B (700 MW, ~2030)**; the bulk — Tarong,
Stanwell, Callide C, Kogan Creek, Millmerran — stretch to **2035–2050**. *Interpretation:* QLD's
coal exit is back-loaded compared with VIC. The dates are *expected* closures and are subject to
revision under state energy policy (the SuperGrid roadmap and its review). *HDRE implication:*
~2.4 GW of coal is slated to leave by 2030, opening a firm, date-certain capacity gap that
renewables + BESS must backfill — a multi-year runway for firming services in QLD.

**繁中.** *脈絡：* 圖 5 依容量與預期除役年排序昆士蘭運轉中的黑煤電廠。*關鍵數據：* 共 **8 座、
約 8,165 MW** 仍在運轉。最早退場者為 **Gladstone（1,680 MW，約 2029 年）** 與
**Callide B（700 MW，約 2030 年）**；主力——Tarong、Stanwell、Callide C、Kogan Creek、
Millmerran——則延至 **2035–2050 年**。*解讀：* 相較維多利亞，昆士蘭的燃煤退場較為後置。這些為
*預期*除役日期，可能隨州能源政策（SuperGrid 路線圖及其檢討）修訂。*對 HDRE 的意涵：* 約 2.4 GW
燃煤預定於 2030 年前退場，開出時程明確的容量缺口，須由再生能源＋電池儲能填補——為昆士蘭的
firming 服務提供長達數年的發展空間。

## Section 6 — Negative Spot-Price Frequency (Fig 6)
## 第六節 — 負電價發生頻率（圖 6）

**EN.** *Context:* Fig 6 counts hours per month where the QLD1 spot price fell below $0/MWh over
the last 12 months — a saturation signal. (Curtailment metrics exist in the API; negative price
is the cleaner, fully-available indicator on the COMMUNITY plan.) *Key statistics:* **1,431 of
8,761 hours (16.3%)** were negative-priced, peaking at **~220 hours in September 2025**.
*Interpretation:* negative prices cluster in daytime spring/summer hours, when solar floods the
market faster than demand or exports can absorb it — clear evidence the QLD grid is *already*
periodically oversupplied at midday, despite only ~32% annual renewable share. *HDRE implication:*
this is the single strongest commercial signal in the chapter: cheap/negative midday energy plus
expensive evening peaks is the exact arbitrage that batteries monetise. QLD's storage runway is
real and quantified.

**繁中.** *脈絡：* 圖 6 統計過去 12 個月 QLD1 現貨價格低於 $0/MWh 的每月時數——一項飽和訊號。
（API 亦提供 curtailment 指標；但在社群版方案下，負電價為更乾淨且完整可得的指標。）*關鍵數據：*
**8,761 小時中有 1,431 小時（16.3%）** 為負電價，於 **2025 年 9 月達高峰約 220 小時**。
*解讀：* 負電價集中於春夏的白天時段，太陽能湧入市場的速度快於需求或外送所能吸收——清楚顯示
即便年再生佔比僅約 32%，昆士蘭電網於日中已*週期性*供給過剩。*對 HDRE 的意涵：* 這是本章最強的
商業訊號：日中廉價／負電價搭配傍晚昂貴尖峰，正是電池套利的標的。昆士蘭的儲能發展空間真實且
可量化。

## Open Questions for the PM Team
## 給專案經理團隊的待釐清問題

The following cannot be answered from API data alone and should be researched against AEMO ISP,
Queensland government, and AEMC sources. 以下無法僅由 API 資料回答，建議比對 AEMO ISP、昆士蘭州
政府與 AEMC 等來源研究：

1. **SuperGrid / renewable roadmap status** — Is QLD's 2030 50%-renewables target still active
   after the LNP government's review, and what is the firmed timeline?
   昆士蘭 2030 年 50% 再生目標在 LNP 政府檢討後是否仍有效？確定時程為何？
2. **CopperString 2032 transmission** — current schedule and budget; how much new renewable
   connection capacity does it actually unlock and when?
   CopperString 2032 輸電線的時程與預算？實際能釋放多少新再生併網容量、何時？
3. **Coal closure firmness** — are the Gladstone (~2029) and Callide B (~2030) dates contractually
   firm, or indicative? What replacement capacity is contracted?
   Gladstone（約 2029）與 Callide B（約 2030）除役日期是合約確定或僅為指示性？已簽約的替代容量為何？
4. **Battery auction outcomes** — results/awarded capacity of QLD's Capacity Investment Scheme and
   state battery tenders (the ~1,887 MW committed battery pipeline — who holds it?).
   昆士蘭容量投資計畫與州級電池招標的得標容量為何？（約 1,887 MW 已承諾電池管線由誰持有？）
5. **Transmission curtailment by zone** — which QLD renewable zones face the worst congestion-driven
   curtailment, beyond the system-wide negative-price signal shown here?
   除本章系統層級負電價訊號外，昆士蘭哪些再生能源區的壅塞性 curtailment 最嚴重？
