# Project Specification: NEM Grid, Market Analysis & Dashboarding

## Project Overview
This project covers the research, analysis, and dashboard development for the National Electricity Market (NEM) grid dynamics, electricity trading volatility, and the impacts of AI data center growth to identify strategic opportunities for HDRE.

## R&D Project: Data Scraper, Analytics & Dashboarding
- **Data Scraping & Processing**: Scrape and analyze network/grid datasets (such as AEMO and NEM data) based on the research items specified below.
- **Dashboard Development**: Build and iterate data dashboards to visualize the key findings of this project, specifically tracking Queensland solar deployment, electricity trading market volatility (Spot & FCAS), and AI data center power demand projections.

#### 2.1.3 Contingency Frequency Control Ancillary Services (Contingency FCAS)
- **Fast Raise**: Increase generation or reduce load within 6 seconds during under-frequency events
- **Fast Lower**: Reduce generation or increase load within 6 seconds during over-frequency events
- **Slow Raise**: Stabilize low frequency within 60 seconds
- **Slow Lower**: Stabilize high frequency within 60 seconds
- **Delayed Raise**: Restore frequency within 5 minutes
- **Delayed Lower**: Restore frequency within 5 minutes
